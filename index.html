How It Began

How We Met

It was an afternoon tea at Martha Cook at the University of Michigan — the kind of event you almost skip, and then don't. Jordan and Jacqueline found each other across the room, and by the time the tea had gone cold, neither wanted to leave. It was, simply, love at first sight.

The Proposal

On Valentine's Day, 2026, Jordan led Jacqueline through the grand halls of the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City. Among centuries of art and quiet grandeur, he asked. She said yes. It was the most beautiful thing either of them had ever seen.
